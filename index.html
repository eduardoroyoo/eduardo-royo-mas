About Me

Currently, I serve as a Relationship Manager at UBS Luxembourg, overseeing platform delivery, fund operations, and post-trade infrastructure for UBS-sponsored funds across multiple jurisdictions. Alongside this, I am pursuing a BSc in Mathematics and Economics through the University of London under the academic direction of the London School of Economics (LSE), focused on formal models of markets, structures, and empirical analysis. My broader academic interest lies in quantitative methods to uncover causality, applied to understanding the determinants of institutional quality. Professionally, I work on the architecture of fund structures and operations and the infrastructure of financial systems.
